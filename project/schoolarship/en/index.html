Schoolarship

Change Ghana. Education is the way to change the community.

Monphy and Youth Foundation was established to enable young people to be an active part of the society through educational means.
To provide necessary skills and knowledge to young people to improve upon themselves as active citizen in the society, and promote and motivate the people to take better participation in community development.
To organize the people for the promotion and progress of their society or community.
Among the steps and progress made since it’s inception include giving scholarships to young brillant students as we offered  to Mr.Patrick Effah a staff and secretary to the foundation when he was a university student in Ghana.

After his graduation, the organization continued sponsoring him by giving him another 2years scholarship to further his studies in Japan and, he was eventually admitted to Tokyo University of Foreign Studies Graduate School to obtain his master’s degree and Currently pursuing his PHD.
He would go back to Ghana to support the society after completing his course and his education has been an inspiration to many people in the community. The foundation Since 2010~2021 have at least, provided scholarships to about 23 students ranging from elementary school to university graduates and to Post graduates (masters) level.

Recently, some of our sponsored students graduated as professional Nurses, Teachers and some are still pursuing their courses. As far as some have graduated and are working to make ends-meet means we are gradually achieving our dreams and objectives.
It is not an easy task in bringing people to this level, but by your concern, cooperation and support you have made it possible.

We can walk to goals and dreams by scholarships. Dora's Message.

Dora Manu, a 13 years student under Monphy & Youth NGO Scholarship or sponsorship has this message for you.

I wish to express my inner-most appreciation for your great love and care towards me and on behalf of some other people who have benefited from your financial support through Mr.Monphy.
We just want to say that We are so much grateful. Is by your financial support which has brought us this far and we are still on course in achieving our dreams and goals of becoming a medical doctors, Engineers, Planners and ICT experts in the near future.
Eight years under the support of Monphy & youth Foundation, I feel safe and secured in my educational pursuit and I am thankful once again for the happiness and smiles that you have brought in my life and that of others.

I will also do my best to help others who would equally need my assistance when I complete school and working. I would want to urge, appeal and encourage you all to continue to support Mr Monphy and his organization so that they can continue to assist more of other needy people in the communities.

I must say again that, your contributions, support and donations are raising future leaders and shaping visions and making people realizing their dreams.
I send my warmest greetings and thanks to all the people of Japan.
