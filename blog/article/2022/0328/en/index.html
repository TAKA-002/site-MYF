Friends donated about 400 brand new basketballs.

Mr.TAKASHI JINDA and Mr.YUMA ADACHI friends donated about 400 brand new basketballs in support of Monphy Foundation.

After it arrived in Ghana, we distributed to various public and private schools at Nkoranza Municipality.
About 200 of the basketballs were donated to various Public senior & Junior high schools and 100 of them were also donated to Monphy Educational Complex and the rest to private schools.
The general Director and staffs at the Educational Directorate and head masters were Present to witness the colorful presentation which took place at the premises of Monphy Educational Complex.

When Takashi was looking for a reliable foundation to donate, he contacts her friend Miss Mizue. Miss Mizue also introduced our organization she met at Otsuka Station. Takashi and Adachi arranged to meet Mr.Monphy for more details. After listening to my side story, Mr.Takaishi accepted the donation to our organization. I am deeply grateful to Takashi and Mizue for trusting the Monphy & Youth Foundation.
