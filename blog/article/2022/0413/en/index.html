Thank you for the donation of clothes.

Kondoh san is among regular donors to Monphy & youth foundation. Last year she helped introduced our foundation to a company searching for a reliable NGO to donate a huge number of brand new T Shirts. Actually, She did all the consultations with the company through to the delivery of the goods at our warehouse at Chiba and all cost borne by her.

The Container was loaded and shipped on 21st of July, 2021 before I left for Ghana for onward distribution.
But many factors, unfortunately caused the delayance in the distribution of the donated items and caused my long stay in Ghana. That included the late arrival and clearance of the container from the ports as expected. The Covid-19 Pandemic which led Japan closed its borders.

After the items arrived at the final destination, The following are some of the places we donated the items. High & Junior High schools, police stations, Fire service station, Professional and unprofessional Soccer teams, that is the coast and some division one teams including some poor and privilege children. All praise and thanks to Kondo san, for the efforts she puts in for the company to donate such a huge bale of clothes and she deserves all the commendations.
Thank you and like Oliver Twist … we ask for more.
